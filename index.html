This portal serves as a helpful bridge between students and professionals. It connects students who have finished 12th grade with people who are already working in various jobs. Our network shared information about students in higher education and experts in different fields. By talking and sharing advice, we help our youth find the right career paths. You can see what others studied and where they work today. Together, we can learn from each other and build a stronger, better future for everyone.
